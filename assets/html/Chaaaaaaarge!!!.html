Force Rules

[-Universal Reactions-]: ()

[Reaction] Counter: A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. For each hit roll from that attack action that misses, allocate 1 damage point to the attacking fighter. For each hit roll of 1, allocate 2 damage points to the attacking fighter instead. ()

[Reaction] Strike Them Down: A fighter can make this reaction when a visible enemy fighter within 1" of them makes a disengage action but before that fighter moves away. Roll a dice. On a 4+, allocate D6 damage points to that enemy fighter. ()

[Reaction] Take Cover: A fighter that is in cover can make this reaction after they are targeted by a missile attack action but before the hit rolls are made. After the hit rolls have been made, roll a dice for each critical hit. On a 4+, that critical hit becomes a hit instead. Fighters with the Mount runemark cannot make this reaction. ()

Selection Rules

[Reaction] Khorne's Due (Khorne Daemons): A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. For each hit roll from that attack action that misses, allocate 2 damage points to the attacking fighter. For each critical hit from that attack action, allocate 1 additional damage point to this fighter. ()

⚁ [Double] Blood for the Blood God (Khorne Daemons): A fighter can only use this ability if an enemy fighter has been taken down by an attack action made by them this activation. This fighter makes a bonus move action or a bonus attack action. ()

⚂ [Triple] Locus of Fury (Khorne Daemons, Hero): A fighter can only use this ability if an enemy fighter has been taken down by an attack action made by them this activation. Until the end of the battle round, add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of melee attack actions made by visible friendly fighters while they are within 6" of this fighter. ()

⚂ [Triple] Murderous Charge (Khorne Daemons, Destroyer): Until the end of this fighter’s activation, the next time this fighter finishes a move action within 1" of an enemy fighter, pick a visible enemy fighter within 1" of this fighter. Allocate a number of damage points to that fighter equal to the value of this ability. ()
