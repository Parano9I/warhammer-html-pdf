Force Rules

[-Universal Reactions-]: ()

[Reaction] Counter: A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. For each hit roll from that attack action that misses, allocate 1 damage point to the attacking fighter. For each hit roll of 1, allocate 2 damage points to the attacking fighter instead. ()

[Reaction] Strike Them Down: A fighter can make this reaction when a visible enemy fighter within 1" of them makes a disengage action but before that fighter moves away. Roll a dice. On a 4+, allocate D6 damage points to that enemy fighter. ()

[Reaction] Take Cover: A fighter that is in cover can make this reaction after they are targeted by a missile attack action but before the hit rolls are made. After the hit rolls have been made, roll a dice for each critical hit. On a 4+, that critical hit becomes a hit instead. Fighters with the Mount runemark cannot make this reaction. ()

Selection Rules

[Reaction] Baffling Parlay (Flesh-eater Courts): A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. Subtract 1 from the Attacks characteristic of that attack action, to a minimum of 1. ()

⚁ [Double] Chosen of the King (Flesh-eater Courts, Elite): A fighter can use this abiltiy only if they are within 6" of a visible friendly fighter with the Flesh-eater Courts faction runemark and both the Hero and Berserker runemarks. Until the end of this fighter's activation, add 2 to the Attacks characteristic of melee attack actions made by this fighter.
⠀ ()

⚁ [Double] Feeding Frenzy (Flesh-eater Courts): A fighter can only use this ability if an enemy fighter has been taken down by an attack action made by them this activation. Remove a number of damage points from this fighter up to the value of this ability.
⠀ ()

⚁ [Double] Skewering Strike (Flesh-eater Courts, Agile): Add 1 to the Stregnth characteristic of the next melee attack action made by this fighter this activation. In addition, if that attack action scores a critical hit, until the end of the battle round, the target fighter cannot make move actions or disengage actions.
⠀ ()

⚂ [Triple] Bringer of Death (Flesh-eater Courts, Hero): Until the end of the battle round, add the value of this ability (rounding up) to the Move characteristic of friendly fighters while they make a move action that starts within 6" of this fighter. ()

⚂ [Triple] Death Scream (Flesh-eater Courts, Agile): Roll a dice for each visible enemy fighter within 8" of this fighter. On a 5, allocate 1 damage point to the fighter being rolled for. On a 6, allocate a number of damage points to the fighter being rolled for equal to the value of this ability.
⠀ ()

⚃ [Quad] The Royal Hunt (Flesh-eater Courts): This fighter makes a bonus move action. Then, they can make a bonus attack action. In addition, add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of that attack action if this fighter is within 1" of a visible friendly fighter.
⠀ ()
