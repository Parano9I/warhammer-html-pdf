Force Rules

[-Universal Reactions-]: ()

[Reaction] Counter: A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. For each hit roll from that attack action that misses, allocate 1 damage point to the attacking fighter. For each hit roll of 1, allocate 2 damage points to the attacking fighter instead. ()

[Reaction] Strike Them Down: A fighter can make this reaction when a visible enemy fighter within 1" of them makes a disengage action but before that fighter moves away. Roll a dice. On a 4+, allocate D6 damage points to that enemy fighter. ()

[Reaction] Take Cover: A fighter that is in cover can make this reaction after they are targeted by a missile attack action but before the hit rolls are made. After the hit rolls have been made, roll a dice for each critical hit. On a 4+, that critical hit becomes a hit instead. Fighters with the Mount runemark cannot make this reaction. ()

Selection Rules

[Reaction] Boon of Fortitude (Slaves to Darkness): A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. Remove 3 damage points allocated to this fighter. ()

⚁ [Double] Imbued with Dark Power (Slaves to Darkness): Until the end of this fighter’s activation, add the value of this ability to the Strength characteristic of melee attack actions made by this fighter. ()

⚂ [Triple] Champion of Darkness (Slaves to Darkness, Hero): A fighter can use this ability only if an enemy fighter has been taken down by an attack action made by them this activation. This fighter makes a bonus move action. Then, they can make a bonus attack action. ()

⚂ [Triple] Knights of Chaos (Slaves to Darkness, Hero, Agile): Until the end of the battle round, add 2 to the Move characteristic of friendly fighters with the Mount runemark and a Toughness characteristic of 5 or more. ()

⚂ [Triple] Trampling Hooves (Slaves to Darkness, Mount): Until the end of this fighter’s activation, the next time this fighter finishes a move action within 1" of an enemy fighter, pick a visible enemy fighter within 1" of this fighter. Allocate a number of damage points to that fighter equal to the value of this ability. ()
