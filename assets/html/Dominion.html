Force Rules

[-Universal Reactions-]: ()

[Reaction] Counter: A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. For each hit roll from that attack action that misses, allocate 1 damage point to the attacking fighter. For each hit roll of 1, allocate 2 damage points to the attacking fighter instead. ()

[Reaction] Strike Them Down: A fighter can make this reaction when a visible enemy fighter within 1" of them makes a disengage action but before that fighter moves away. Roll a dice. On a 4+, allocate D6 damage points to that enemy fighter. ()

[Reaction] Take Cover: A fighter that is in cover can make this reaction after they are targeted by a missile attack action but before the hit rolls are made. After the hit rolls have been made, roll a dice for each critical hit. On a 4+, that critical hit becomes a hit instead. Fighters with the Mount runemark cannot make this reaction. ()

 ⚃ [Quad] Rampage: This fighter can make a bonus move action. Then, they can make a bonus attack action.

Selection Rules

[Reaction] Thunderous Departure (Thunderstrike Stormcasts): A fighter can make this reaction when they are targeted by a melee attack action, after the damage is totalled but before it is allocated to this fighter, if it is enough for this fighter to be taken down. Allocate D6 damage points to their attacker. Fighters with the Beast runemark or both the Scout and Fly runemarks cannot make this reaction. ()

⚁ [Double] Blaze of Glory (Thunderstrike Stormcasts): A fighter can only use this ability if they have 15 or more damage points allocated to them. This fighter makes a bonus attack action. ()

⚁ [Double] Shock and Awe (Thunderstrike Stormcasts, Hero): A fighter can only use this ability if an enemy fighter has been taken down by an attack action made by this fighter this activation. This fighter makes a bonus move action or a bonus attack action. ()

⚂ [Triple] Coordinated Strike (Thunderstrike Stormcasts, Hero, Champion): Pick a number of visible friendly fighters equal to half the value of this ability (rounding up) within 9" of this fighter. Those fighters can each make a bonus move action or a bonus attack action (some can make a bonus move action and others can make a bonus attack action). ()
