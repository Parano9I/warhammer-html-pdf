Force Rules

[-Universal Reactions-]: ()

[Reaction] Counter: A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. For each hit roll from that attack action that misses, allocate 1 damage point to the attacking fighter. For each hit roll of 1, allocate 2 damage points to the attacking fighter instead. ()

[Reaction] Strike Them Down: A fighter can make this reaction when a visible enemy fighter within 1" of them makes a disengage action but before that fighter moves away. Roll a dice. On a 4+, allocate D6 damage points to that enemy fighter. ()

[Reaction] Take Cover: A fighter that is in cover can make this reaction after they are targeted by a missile attack action but before the hit rolls are made. After the hit rolls have been made, roll a dice for each critical hit. On a 4+, that critical hit becomes a hit instead. Fighters with the Mount runemark cannot make this reaction. ()

Selection Rules

[Reaction] Thunderous Departure (Sacrosanct Chamber): A fighter can make this reaction when they are targeted by a melee attack action, after the damage is totalled but before it is allocated to this fighter, if it is enough for this fighter to be taken down. Allocate D6 damage points to their attacker. Fighters with the Beast runemark or both the Scout and Fly runemarks cannot make this reaction. ()

⚁ [Double] Channelled Empowerment (Sacrosanct Chamber): Add the value of this ability to the Strength characteristic of the next melee attack action made by this fighter in this activation. Fighters with the Beast runemark cannot use this ability. ()

⚁ [Double] Cleanse the Realm of Taint (Sacrosanct Chamber, Hero): A fighter can only use this ability if an enemy fighter has been taken down by an attack action made by them this activation. This fighter makes a bonus move action or a bonus attack action. ()

⚂ [Triple] Shatter Spirit Flask (Sacrosanct Chamber, Hero, Berserker): Allocate a number of damage points equal to the value of this ability to this fighter and to all visible fighters within 3" of this fighter. ()

⚃ [Quad] Thunderous Pounce (Sacrosanct Chamber, Ferocious): Until the end of this fighter’s activation, the next time this fighter finishes a move action within 1" of an enemy fighter, pick a visible enemy fighter within 1" of this fighter. Allocate a number of damage points to that fighter equal to double the value of this ability. ()
